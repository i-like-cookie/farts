Boring Scientific Facts About Farts

Farts, scientifically known as flatulence, are caused by the accumulation of gases in the digestive system. These gases are byproducts of the digestion process and swallowed air. The average person passes gas about 14 times a day, releasing between 500 to 1,500 milliliters of gas.
